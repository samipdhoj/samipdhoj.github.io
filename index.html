Tour: Everest Base Camp Trek

The Everest Base Camp Trek, also known as the EBC Trek, is an all-inclusive 14-day trekking package from Kathmandu, Nepal, to the base camp of the highest mountain in the world. The walking tour is high on the bucket list of things to do for adventurers around the globe. It’s because the hike to Everest Base Camp allows you to see a magnificent close-up view of towering Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world, from every angle.

Estimated days of hiking: 5 days
Difficulty: Hard
Maximum elevation: 4200 mts
